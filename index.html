Max McCormick

Composer for games and film

I’m a composer based in Berlin, working across games, film, and electronic music.

With a background in electronic engineering, my work often sits between music and systems.

I recently completed an adaptive score for Yerba Buena (Mad About Pandas), a puzzle-platforming adventure set in 1970s San Francisco.
